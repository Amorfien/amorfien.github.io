Frequently Asked Questions about pixel2stitch

How to achieve the best generation results?

Use third-party graphics editors for images before uploading them to pixel2stitch. Perform cropping, add contrast and vibrancy to colors. Remove the background if necessary.

Is there compatibility with other manufacturers' editors?

No, at the moment you cannot use third-party editors to work with schemes generated in pixel2stitch.

Does image processing happen on my device?

Yes, your images and schemes are not transmitted anywhere, they are processed and stored locally on your device.

Does pixel2stitch work without internet access?

Yes, it works. But for generating new schemes, the application may ask you to watch an advertisement, which requires an internet connection.

What should I do if I cannot watch ads?

In this case, the generation counter will reset automatically after one week. You can also reset the counter by deleting the application and reinstalling it, but in this case you will lose all your saved schemes.
